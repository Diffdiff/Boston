üéí New England Fall Road Trip Packing List

üëï Clothing

  • Base layers (long-sleeve moisture-wicking shirts)
  • Fleece or mid-weight sweater
  • Insulated jacket (temps can drop fast at elevation)
  • Rain jacket or shell (New England is moody)
  • Hiking pants and casual jeans
  • Daytime shoes + hiking boots with ankle support
  • Cozy socks (wool = warm and stink-resistant)
  • Sleepwear and loungewear
  • 1‚Äì2 nice outfits for dinners in Boston or Newport

üß¢ Accessories

  • Beanie/hat for warmth
  • Lightweight gloves (great for cold mornings or summits)
  • Sunglasses (even fall sun hits hard above tree line)

üß∞ Gear & Hiking Essentials

  • Headlamp (just in case, esp. for Mt. Washington)
  • Trekking poles (optional but useful for descents)
  • Trail snacks and electrolytes
  • Basic first-aid kit (blister pads, Advil, etc.)
  • Phone battery bank
  • Map of Mt. Washington trail (offline version!)
  • Dry bag (if you're bringing gear on the Saco River)

üì∏ Fun & Tech

  • Camera or phone with extra storage
  • Binoculars (for birds or distant peaks)
  • Playlist downloaded for offline listening
  • Journal or field notes (optional, but always cool)

üìÑ Travel Docs & Extras

  • ID/passport
  • Flight + lodging confirmations
  • National Park Pass or parking cash for state parks
  • Snacks for car rides
  • Book or Kindle (for evening wind-down)
